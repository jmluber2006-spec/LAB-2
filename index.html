Hello, I am John Mark Luber

My hobbies include:

  • Making and serving coffee as a barista
  • Attending church activities and services
  • Helping and serving other people
  • Art and listening to music
  • Spending time improving myself and my skills

Some people chase dreams, but I wake up every day ready to work hard for mine. As a barista, I take pride in serving each cup with care, precision, and a genuine smile that brightens someone's day. My role behind the counter has taught me patience, responsibility, and the value of consistency. As a devoted member of the Church of Christ, my faith shapes my character and strengthens my commitment to live with integrity and compassion. I believe that success is built on discipline and perseverance, so I give my best effort in every task entrusted to me. Through dedication, faith, and determination, I continue striving to grow into the best version of myself.
